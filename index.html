Cat

"The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domestic cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family. It was long thought(as 3100 BC) that cat domestication was initiated in ancient Egypt."
